# Case Studies 

This section contains practical applications of systematic trading strategies
across different markets, with the aim of evaluating, optimizing,
and discussing their empirical performance.

Each case study follows a structured workflow:

- preliminary analysis of raw data, including cleaning and dataset adjustments  
- execution of the selected trading strategies  
- parameter optimization and performance evaluation  
- discussion of results and identification of the best-fit strategy for the specific market
